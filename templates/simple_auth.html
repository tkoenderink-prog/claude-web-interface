How to get your Session Key:

  1. Open claude.ai in your browser
  2. Log in with your Claude Max 20 account
  3. Open Developer Tools (F12 or right-click → Inspect)
  4. Go to the Application/Storage tab
  5. Find Cookies → claude.ai
  6. Look for cookie named "sessionKey"
  7. Copy the Value (a long string)
  8. Paste it in the Session Key field above

Your session key is like a temporary password that proves you're logged in. It expires after a few days, so you'll need to update it occasionally.

Alternative Method:

  1. While on claude.ai, open Console in Developer Tools
  2. Type: document.cookie.match(/sessionKey=([^;]+)/)[1]
  3. Press Enter and copy the result
